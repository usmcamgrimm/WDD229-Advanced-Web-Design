There are more than 100 hotels in the Oslo area. Ranging from cheap and economic to extravagent 5-star luxury, Oslo has a hotel for every budget. On one end of the spectrum, the Citybox hotel offers rooms starting at $59, while the luxurious Thief has rooms starting at $313.

Oslo has over 300 Airbnb offerings. Just like the hotels in Oslo, prices for an Airbnb differ greatly, from cheap to expensive. There is, however, one common trend among Oslo's Airbnb properties: among the 150,000 guest reviews, they average 4.8 / 5 stars.
